Our pipes are known for high strength, dimensional accuracy, and long service life, making them suitable for pressure, structural, and fluid transmission applications.


Carbon Steel Seamless Pipes
Carbon Steel Seamless Pipes are produced without any welding joint, offering superior strength, uniform structure, and excellent resistance to high pressure and temperature. These pipes are widely used in critical and high-temperature services.


Carbon Steel Welded Pipes (ERW / EFW / SAW)
Carbon Steel Welded Pipes are manufactured by forming steel plates or coils and welding them longitudinally or spirally. These pipes offer excellent dimensional accuracy, cost-effectiveness, and reliable performance, ideal for structural and medium-pressure applications.
Low Carbon Steel Pipes – Seamless & Welded
Low Carbon Steel Pipes are preferred for their excellent weldability, toughness, and formability, suitable for general-purpose, low-temperature, and fabrication applications.
API Line Pipes – Seamless & Welded
